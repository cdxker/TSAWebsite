2018-2019 Texas TSA Scholarships

The Texas TSA Scholarships are reserved for current TSA members graduating as seniors in the spring. Awardeds are handed out each school year as followed:

  • One $2,500 scholarship will be awarded to students planning to teach in a CTE/STEM field.
  • One $2,500 scholarship will be awarded to a student planning on going into teaching. In the event there are no candidates that are entering into these fields, the candidate(s) with the highest rating in TX TSA scholarship rating system will receive one or both of these scholarships.
  • One of the above scholarships will be awarded in the memory of W.A. Mayfield founding father of Texas TSA and the other will be given in the memory of Al Seher.
  • Six $1,500 scholarships will be awarded to a student majoring in any CTE/STEM related field in an approved post-secondary school.
  • One of the above scholarships will be awarded in the memory of George “Big George” Howle and one other will be given in honor of the founding fathers of ATTE (Association of Texas Technology Education) - The Greer/Mays/Wright Industrial Arts Memorial Scholarship.

William P. Elrod Memorial

In honor of the Board of Directors William P. Elrod, a founding father of TSA from Madison, KS. Mr. Elrod, who died in 1990. The amount awarded for this scholarship is $2,500 eligible candidates include TSA members from one of two categories: Graduating high school seniors or TSA alumni/alumnae who are enrolled in an undergraduate program or a career/technical school. Including have a high school or college or career/technical school GPA of 3.0 or better and be able to describe a financial need for the scholarship.

CompTIA

CompTIA scholarships awards money to outstanding senior students within the TSA chapter who has been in the organization for two years and met certain criteria. Such as, GPA requirement of 2.8 or higher, participated in a community service project,and attended either a state or national leadership conference that following year. Ten students are then chosen and notified by June 1 that they recieve $2,500 for school.

National TSA Alumni Association Collegiate Scholarship Application

Scholarship program which provides financial support to those recognized for their hard work and dedication as a TSA alumni who work continuously to give back to TSA even though their time as a competitor has passed. The amount awarded for this scholarship is $1,000 and will be distributed directly to the recipient's college or university's financial aid department.

Requirements:
  • Applicant must be enrolled as a full-time student working towards the completion of an undergraduate degree.
  • Applicant must have one semester remaining of their undergraduate degree to be eligible.
  • Applicant must have a minimum G.P.A. of 2.5.
  • Applicant must be a member of the National TSA Alumni Association.
  • Applicant demonstrates involvement in TSA since becoming an alumni.
  • Applicant must submit a completed application packet with the required deliverables by the deadline stated in the timelines section. Please only submit one application packet with all required deliverables.
  • Applicant may not be currently serving as a member of the National TSA Alumni Council.
