Pathways

John A Dubiski Career High School is a GPISD public high school of choice with focuses on equipping students with the skills they need for professions in the 21st century. Students choose a sequential course of study designed to prepare them for a 2-year, 4-year, or technical college. In addition to their high school diploma, students are also eligible to complete programs that award them with advanced certifications, industry training, internship, and career-field experiences. Dubiski offers HB5 endorsements including STEM, Business & Industry, and Public Service. However, we are focusing on the pathways related to TSA and technology.

The programs we offer are:

  • Engineering
  • Aerospace Academy
  • Graphic Communication
  • Architecture
  • Automotive Technology
  • Information Technology
  • Media Technology
